Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler.

This is the main entrance of all works of Zhang Chang-kai, including projects, articles and comments. For a long time, Zhang Chang-kai has been working on establishing logically self-complete physical theories. Motivated by this origin, he has accomplished skeletons from basic Mathematics towards classical gauge field theory, and is now working with hope to extend it into the quantum area.

Research Interest

Recently, I am attracted by conducting mathematical investigation of various aspects of Quantum Field Theory, High Energy Physics, as well as candidates of a Quantum Gravity Theory. Currently, I am working on establishing logically rigorous formulation of Quantum Field Theory under path integral formalism.
